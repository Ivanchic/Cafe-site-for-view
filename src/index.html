The French baguette ( French une baguette de pain or simply une baguette , feminine; pain français among the Belgians and Quebecers) is a long and thin baked product , soft inside , with a crispy crust , often dusted with flour . A standard baguette has a length of approximately 65 cm, a width of 5-6 cm and a height of 3-4 cm. Weight is approximately 250 g. One of the typical products of French cuisine and a unique symbol of France . In 2022, the French baguette is recognized as an intangible cultural heritage by UNESCO
